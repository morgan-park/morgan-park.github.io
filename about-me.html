Who am I?

I am a women in tech evangelist who loves to learn new things and challenging problems. I was born and raised in Seoul, South Korea, and moved to the United States in 2013. I am currently a graduate student in Master's program of Computer Science at Tufts University. My interest centers on Data Science, Machine Learning, and Cloud Development.
